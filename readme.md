## Daoist Pharmaceutical 

In this game, diseases spread over a map of asia (Non-middle east & Russia). The player acts as the son of a god that has been sent down to heal the lands. The player will have access to medications that they can spread through the game, these medications can be bought through the market class.

Personal contribution; The market class, has 6 options. 3 tiers of eastern medications, and 3 tiers of western midecations, these medications go up in price and rarity in shop. Even within the same tier, certain medcations will have different chances of getting picked, the price random range is set per tier.
